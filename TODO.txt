//===---------------------------------------------------------------------===//

Lexer-related diagnostics should point to the problematic character, not the
start of the token.  For example:

int y = 0000\
00080;

diag.c:4:9: error: invalid digit '8' in octal constant
int y = 0000\
        ^

should be:

diag.c:4:9: error: invalid digit '8' in octal constant
00080;
   ^
